Ashgabat

General secondary education is compulsory and consists of ten years of study. Secondary school eductaion is free throughout the country. Secondary school students study according to a national curriculm in the Turkmen or Russian language, but many take English as a second language. The quality of eductaion students recieve is considered poor at best because most teachers are unmotivated, and many students to not take education serious.

  • Primary Education: 3 years, Grade 1-3, Age level-7-10
  • Secondary Education: 7 years, Grade 4-10, Age level-10-16 Certificate/ diploma awarded: State Examination for School Leaving Certificate.
  • Specialized Secondary: Colleges and Technical Schools, 2 years, Age level 16-18 Certificate/diploma awarded: Diploma of Specialized Secondary Education
  • Vocational Secondary: Vocational Lyceum and College, 1 year, Age level 16-17 Certificate/diploma awarded: Diploma
